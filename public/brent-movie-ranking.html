My Top Movies

By Brent

Fantastic Mr. Fox

A charming story of a fox who wants a better life for his family.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

A man whose world has become so bland that his imagination begins to blur with reality
finds himself on an unexpected adventure beyond his daydreams.

La La Land

An aspiring actress and jazz musician fall in love but when their dreams begin to take them
in seperate directions, their love is put to the test.
